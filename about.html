MY JOURNEY

I studied at Christuraja Matriculation Higher Secondary School in Marthandam for 14 years and completed my SSLC and HSC public examinations there. After completing my schooling, I started studying programming courses at the CADD Center, where I learned C, C++, Python, and completed a Diploma in Python. During these courses, I worked on several projects, including a grading system for marks in C++, a flight booking website for administrators in Django, and received certificates for my achievements. In 2022, I joined Ponjesly College of Engineering, where I pursued a degree in Computer Science & Engineering and graduated in 2026. I have the ability to design simple and responsive web pages that interact well with users. Whatever I do, I strive to apply my knowledge to the best of my ability. Thank you!
